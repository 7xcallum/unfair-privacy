# Privacy Policy for Unfair

**Last updated: 11 May 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Unfair (the "app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application and the underlying briefing service.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **marrinercallum@gmail.com**.

## Who we are

Unfair is operated by Callum Marriner, a sole proprietor based in Australia. Unfair provides personalised daily intelligence briefings delivered through an iOS application.

## What information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver your daily briefing and operate the service.

**Information you give us directly**

- Your email address (used to sign you in and identify your account)
- Your first name (used in the briefing greeting)
- Profile information you provide during onboarding: your role, industry, location, what you do, your financial goal, what you want briefs about, and what content you already consume
- Optional weekly check-in responses (your current focus, momentum, energy, what you are working on, blockers)

**Information collected automatically**

- A Supabase user identifier (a random UUID) created when you sign in
- Your subscription status and expiration date (provided by Apple via RevenueCat)
- Records of which briefings have been generated for your account, including their dates and word counts

**Information we do not collect**

- We do not collect your precise location.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, or camera.
- We do not use third-party advertising identifiers and we do not display ads.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.

## How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

- Deliver your daily briefing tailored to your profile
- Send you a push notification when a new briefing is ready
- Verify and manage your subscription
- Improve the quality of the briefing through aggregated, non-identifying analysis
- Respond to your support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for advertising purposes.

## Who we share information with

We share information only with service providers strictly necessary to run the app:

- **Supabase** (database hosting) — stores your account, profile, and briefing history
- **Anthropic** (AI model provider) — generates your briefing using your profile inputs; Anthropic does not retain your data for training
- **Apple** (App Store and push notifications) — processes subscriptions and delivers notifications
- **RevenueCat** (subscription receipt management) — validates and syncs your subscription status
- **OneSignal** (push delivery) — receives a notification identifier so we can deliver push messages to your device

Each provider operates under their own privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

## Data location and retention

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile and briefing history are permanently deleted within thirty days, except where we are required to retain records for legal, billing, or fraud-prevention purposes.

## Your rights

You can at any time:

- Access the information we hold about you by emailing us
- Correct or update your profile information from inside the app
- Export your data by emailing us
- Delete your account by emailing us; deletion is permanent within thirty days

Residents of the European Union, the United Kingdom, and California have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA respectively. Contact us to exercise those rights.

## Children

Unfair is not intended for and not directed to anyone under the age of sixteen. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and credentials stored in the iOS Keychain. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information safe and respond promptly to any incident.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notifying you in the app.
